Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and billing process.

How is the "per page" cost calculated for certified translations?

For certified translations, the industry standard defines a "page" as up to 250 words. If your document has dense text exceeding 250 words, it will be calculated proportionally. There are no hidden fees for complex formatting.

Are there any hidden fees for notarization or physical mailing?

Digital delivery of your certified translation is always free and typically universally accepted. If you specifically require physical notarization or hard copies mailed via FedEx, these are available as optional add-ons during checkout.

Do you offer discounts for bulk documents?

Yes. If you are translating a large portfolio of documents (over 20 pages), please contact our enterprise team. We offer volume-based discounts and dedicated account management for bulk orders.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, and wire transfers for corporate accounts. All payments are processed securely via Stripe.
